Encrypted Storage

Secure your files with strong encryption—locally or in the cloud.

Why Use Encrypted Storage?

Encrypted storage ensures your personal files, documents, and data are protected from unauthorized access. Whether stored on your device or in the cloud, encryption keeps your information safe—even if it falls into the wrong hands.

Recommended Tools

  • VeraCrypt: Open-source tool for creating encrypted volumes on your device.
  • NordLocker: Secure cloud and local encrypted file storage.
  • Cryptomator: Encrypts your cloud storage folders (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.).

Tips

  • Always use a strong master password.
  • Don’t share encryption keys or credentials with untrusted sources.
  • Backup your encrypted volumes securely.

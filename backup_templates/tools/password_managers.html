Password Managers

Secure your online accounts by managing strong, unique passwords.

What is a Password Manager?

A password manager helps you generate, retrieve, and store complex passwords for your online accounts. It encrypts all your passwords in a secure vault, protected by a single master password.

Top Benefits:

  • Generate strong, unique passwords
  • Auto-fill login credentials securely
  • Store sensitive information like notes and payment info
  • Access passwords across devices with syncing

Recommended Tools:

  • LastPass
  • 1Password
  • Bitwarden

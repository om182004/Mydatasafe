Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Double your login protection with an extra layer of security.

Why Use 2FA?

Two-Factor Authentication adds an extra step to your login process by requiring a second form of verification like a code sent to your phone or an authenticator app. Even if someone has your password, they can’t log in without the second factor.

Popular 2FA Apps

  • Google Authenticator
  • Authy
  • Microsoft Authenticator
  • Duo Security

Tips

  • Enable 2FA wherever available – email, social media, banking, etc.
  • Use an authenticator app instead of SMS when possible for stronger security.
  • Keep backup codes safe in case you lose your phone.

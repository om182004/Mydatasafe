Data Cleaners

Remove traces of your digital activity across platforms.

What are Data Cleaners?

Data cleaners are tools that help you remove unused accounts, cookies, browsing history, cache, and personal data from online and local platforms.

Popular Tools:

  • CCleaner: Wipes browser history and junk files.
  • JustDeleteMe: Helps you find and delete old accounts.
  • BleachBit: Securely deletes unnecessary files and protects privacy.

Why Use Them?

Data cleaners improve privacy, speed up your device, and reduce the chance of personal information leaks.

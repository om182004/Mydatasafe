Private Browsers

Keep your web activity private and avoid tracking

Why Use Private Browsers?

Private browsers help prevent trackers, cookies, and ads from collecting data about your browsing behavior.

Popular Private Browsers:

  • Brave: Blocks ads and trackers automatically.
  • Firefox Focus: Erases history after each session and blocks trackers.
  • DuckDuckGo Browser: Includes built-in tracker blocking and encryption enforcement.

Benefits:

  • Reduces targeted ads
  • Improves browsing speed
  • Limits personal data exposure

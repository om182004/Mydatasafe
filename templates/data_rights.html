Your Data Rights

Understand your rights under modern data privacy laws.

Right to Access

You can request access to the data a company has collected about you.

Right to Erasure

You can ask to have your personal data erased (also known as the right to be forgotten).

Right to Data Portability

You have the right to transfer your data to another service provider.

Right to Object

You can object to how your data is being used, such as for marketing purposes.

Right to Restrict Processing

You can limit how organizations use your data while a request is being considered.
